Axelar integrates Solana mainnet, enabling cross-chain asset transfers and messaging between Solana and Axelar-supported chains including Ethereum, XRP Ledger, Stellar, and Hedera. The integration gives developers, asset issuers, and protocols a secure and reliable path to connect Solana with the broader multichain ecosystem.
